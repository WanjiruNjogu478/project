    Frequently Asked Questions

    What are your operating hours?

    We are open Monday to Friday from 8 AM to 10 PM, and on Saturday from 9 AM to 4 PM.

    Where are you located?

    Our salon is located in Nairobi, Kenya. You can find us at Gataka, Nairobi.

    How can I contact you?

    You can reach us by phone at +254 707 280 732 or by email at rawannjogu@gmail.com.

    How do I book an appointment?

    You can book an appointment online through our website or by calling us directly.

    Do I need to pay a deposit to secure my appointment?

    Yes, we require a small deposit to secure your appointment, which can be paid via M-Pesa.

    What services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of services including hair services, skin care, nail services, makeup services, waxing and hair removal, massage therapy, body treatments, and eyelash and eyebrow services.
